This is a tool that can be embedded on any website (e.g. an organization website, a blog, a news site) in a single line of code:

The point is to help people onboard the climate movement. When users submit the form, they can receive:

  • An automatic email introducing them to local direct action organizers (e.g. local XR chapter, Declare Emergency, mutual aid groups)
  • Guidance for leading transitions in their own communities. For example, emails focused on things like:
    • Gathering with neighbors and building community
    • Transitioning to car-free places
    • Building food/energy/water resilience
    • Political mobilization
    • Systems change
  • Any other information, guides, resources that could be useful.

This lifts all boats and benefits all groups that participate. If a New Yorker visits a Los Angeles website and fills out the form, it will connect the reader with relevant resources for New York. If an Angeleno visits a NY website and fills out the form, it will connect the reader with relevant resources for LA.
